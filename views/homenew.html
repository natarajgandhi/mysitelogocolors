Who we are and what we do

South Jersey Tamil Association (SJTA) is a volunteer-based, non-profit, charitable, secular organization. It is organized to promote Tamil education among the children in New Jersey of the United States of America.

A SJTA volunteer has been teaching Tamil for the last 10 years. Tamil classes are conducted in every Friday for ages 4 and above kids. Based on the age and Tamil proficiency of the children, various levels of classes are organized and offered.
